How It Works

Scrubb.it silently intercepts image uploads in your browser. The extension redraws the image via the Canvas API, which strips all EXIF metadata such as GPS coordinates, timestamps, device info, and more. The cleaned image is then passed to the website automatically.

Privacy

Scrubb.it runs entirely in your browser — no image ever leaves your device. All metadata is removed locally, ensuring full privacy and security.
